# Spurtle Seizes the Day

A PyWeek 41 entry by **Large Sandwich Models** (cyhawk, Alex, hayalci, nchris,
jmc88, K2, shainer).

## The story

We went onto this planet looking for a place where we could let our civilization
thrive, but instead found only misery. What we didn't know is that, at
nightfall, the dark that envelopes you has a way to drive people insane, and the
only way to preserve your mind is to stay in the light.

But then we met **Spurtle**. A gentle alien creature, we named it so owing to
its spider-turtle look. We built our city on its back, and Spurtle goes where we
want it to go, allowing us to be on the move at all times.

However, both Spurtle and us need to feed in order to survive. We eat
**grains**, while Spurtle likes to munch on **trees**. Hopefully, we can get
what we need quickly enough, before too many of us succumb to the night-induced
**madness**...

Maybe, if we stay alive long enough, we'll have a chance to develop the
technology we need to make sure we are kept safe from the maddening sounds of
the night!

## Game mechanics

### Goal

Your goal is to survive long enough to buy all available upgrades.

### Controls

- **Arrow keys**: move Spurtle around.
- **Space bar**: harvest the nearest resource.
- **Upgrades button**: click on it to acquire upgrades.
